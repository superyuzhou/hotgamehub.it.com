Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters and collecting power-ups to become stronger.

As you progress, the difficulty increases, and you'll need to strategically choose upgrades to counter the growing monster threat. Will you focus on offensive abilities, defensive skills, or movement speed?

With its addictive gameplay loop and roguelike elements, Monster Survivors offers a unique experience every time you play!

How to Play

1

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.

2

Your character attacks automatically when monsters are in range.

3

Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones.

4

Survive as long as possible and defeat as many monsters as you can!

Pro Tips & Strategies

✓

Keep moving: Standing still is the fastest way to get overwhelmed by monsters. Develop a pattern of movement to avoid getting cornered.

✓

Balance your upgrades: Don't focus solely on damage. A good mix of offensive and defensive abilities will help you survive longer.

✓

Prioritize experience gems: Collect experience gems whenever possible to level up faster and gain more abilities.

✓

Learn enemy patterns: Different monsters have different movement and attack patterns. Learning these will help you anticipate and avoid danger.
